      FREQUENTRLY ASKED QUESTIONS
      Who is VoltEstimate?

      VoltEstimate is a trusted electrical estimating company offering professional cost estimation, project management, and drafting services to support contractors and businesses worldwide. We help both small and large firms confidently bid on projects with accurate, timely, and competitive estimates.

      Since you're based in NYC, do you also work with clients in other cities across the U.S.?

      Absolutely! We proudly serve clients nationwide and partner with companies across the U.S.

      What Experience does the International Estimating Team Have?

      The VoltEstimate team brings decades of combined experience in electrical estimating, project management, and construction support. Our certified estimators and engineers have worked on a wide range of residential, commercial, industrial, and low-voltage projects across the U.S. and internationally. We’ve partnered with general contractors, subcontractors, and developers, delivering accurate estimates that help win bids and keep projects on track.

      ABOUT US

      Who We Are

      “Code-Compliant, Cost-Effective, and Experience-Driven Estimating”
      Our estimators bring deep expertise in NEC and NFPA 70 codes, ensuring that every estimate meets all state-mandated standards while incorporating the most cost-efficient materials, devices, and cable types approved by code. From residential homes and apartment complexes to commercial buildings, hospitals, schools, airports, and industrial facilities—we’ve successfully handled a diverse range of projects.
      We deliver estimates with competitive market pricing, while also factoring in union/non-union rates and NECA labor units—customized to the project's complexity and scope. With us, you're not just getting numbers; you're getting a smart, code-compliant, and dependable estimating solution tailored to your specific needs.
