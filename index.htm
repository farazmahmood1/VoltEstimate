        ABOUT US

        Who We Are

        Electrical Take-offs VoltEstimate specialises in delivering precise and dependable electrical estimating solutions. We focus on providing material takeoffs that align with your project goals, timelines, and budget. From individual contractors to large construction companies, we cater to all scales of work with the same level of accuracy, speed, and professionalism.
        At the core of our estimating services is precision and efficiency. We leverage industry-leading software including PlanSwift 11.0, Bluebeam, and Excel to deliver detailed material takeoffs with accurate, market-based pricing. For more specialized needs, we offer customized estimating solutions using advanced tools such as Trimble Accubid Classic with LiveCount, McCormick, ConEst, and Electrical Bid Manager (EBM).
